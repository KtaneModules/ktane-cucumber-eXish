On the Subject of Cucumber

“Cucumber!” - Kavin

On the module is a cucumber. You must eat the cucumber.

To take a bite out of the cucumber simply press it. After 5 bites you will have full ingested the cucumber, and the module will solve.

However you cannot take bites out of the cucumber too quickly, otherwise the sheer amount of cucumber you are taking in will choke you and give you a strike. Wait at least 30 seconds (bomb time) between each bite for your own safety.

If the bomb’s starting time is less than 2 minutes, then you are taking part in a cucumber eating contest and need to stuff the cucumber directly down your throat. To do this simply press the cucumber as if you were going to take a bite.
